Dr. Abhishek Tiwari

Associate Professor, Maersk Mc-Kinney Moller Institute, University of Southern Denmark.

My research sits at the intersection of programming languages and software engineering, with a strong focus on static program analyses (program slicing, language-based security, information flow control) and privacy for Android. (Content migrated from original Google Sites.)

  • B.Tech, SASTRA University

Current & recent positions

  • Associate Professor — Maersk Mc-Kinney Moller Institute, SDU Odense.

Research Interests

Static program analyses, program slicing, information flow security, language-based security, privacy on Android, multilingual program analysis, automated repair.
